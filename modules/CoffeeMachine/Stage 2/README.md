# Coffee Machine

## Stage 2

In this stage you should be able to use existing ingrediant for making coffee

inital amount of componets are:
Coffe beans 400 g
Sugar 300 g
Milk 100 ml
Water 2000 ml
10 cups

for making one portion of coffe you need:
1 cup
10g of sugar
30g of coffee
50ml of water
10 ml of milk
